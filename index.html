Our approach

Made with care,
every time

We believe a bake made just for you should feel genuinely different — in taste, in presentation, and in the care that's been put into it. Everything we make starts with a conversation.

01

All made from scratch

No pre-mixes, no shortcuts. Every sponge, filling, and decoration is prepared fresh in our home kitchen using quality ingredients.

02

Dietary requirements welcomed

Gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan — we're happy to work with dietary needs so nobody has to miss out on something wonderful.

03

Your brief, your bake

We don't do generic. Bring us a colour, a theme, a feeling — and we'll translate it into something edible and beautiful.

04

Local, personal, and friendly

We're a small, passionate operation. You'll always speak to the baker directly — no customer service forms, no waiting around.

How a custom order works

Get in touch

Drop us a message with your occasion, date, and any ideas you have in mind. No formal brief needed — a few words is plenty to start.

We'll chat through it

We'll talk flavour, design, size, and any dietary requirements. This is where the fun part starts — and where we make it yours.

Quote & booking

You'll receive a clear quote for your order. Once you're happy, a deposit secures your slot on the calendar.

We bake it fresh

Your order is made to order in the days before your event. Collected fresh or locally delivered — ready to delight.
